Our Teaching Approach

At Sunshine English School, we believe in a holistic approach to education that nurtures not just academic excellence but also character and life skills.

Experiential Learning

We encourage learning through hands-on activities, experiments, and real-world applications to make concepts more tangible and memorable.

Collaborative Environment

Our classrooms foster teamwork, communication, and mutual respect through group projects and peer learning exercises.

Critical Thinking

Character Development

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the student-teacher ratio at Sunshine English School?

We maintain a student-teacher ratio of 25:1 to ensure personalized attention and quality education for every student. Additional teaching assistants are available for primary classes to provide extra support.

Does the school offer any extracurricular activities?

Yes, we offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (cricket, basketball, volleyball), performing arts (music, dance, drama), visual arts, debate, robotics, and various clubs to nurture diverse talents and interests.

What board curriculum does the school follow?

Sunshine English School follows the Maharashtra State Board curriculum. We focus on comprehensive coverage of the syllabus with additional enrichment programs to enhance learning beyond textbooks.

Are there remedial classes for students who need extra help?

Yes, we provide remedial classes after school hours for students who need additional support in specific subjects. Our teachers identify learning gaps and provide targeted assistance to ensure no student is left behind.

How does the school assess student progress?

We follow a continuous evaluation system that includes regular formative assessments, project work, class participation, term examinations, and comprehensive feedback. Progress reports are shared with parents quarterly along with parent-teacher meetings.
