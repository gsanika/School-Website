Our Curriculum

A comprehensive approach to education that balances academic excellence with holistic development

Holistic Curriculum Framework

Our curriculum is designed to provide a balanced education that not only focuses on academic excellence but also nurtures the physical, emotional, and social development of students. We follow the Maharashtra State Board curriculum with additional enrichment programs that prepare students for future challenges.

The curriculum incorporates modern teaching methodologies, project-based learning, and digital resources to make learning engaging and effective. Our approach encourages critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity while maintaining academic rigor.

"Education is not preparation for life; education is life itself." At Sunshine English School, we believe in creating learning experiences that are meaningful, relevant, and connected to real-life situations.

The curriculum is regularly reviewed and updated to incorporate the latest educational research and best practices. We ensure that our students are well-prepared for the challenges of the 21st century while remaining rooted in values and traditions.

Assessment System

Our comprehensive assessment framework designed to monitor progress and provide meaningful feedback

Continuous Assessment

Regular formative assessments conducted throughout the year to monitor progress and provide timely feedback. These include class tests, quizzes, projects, and participation in classroom activities.

Term Examinations

Comprehensive summative assessments conducted at the end of each term to evaluate overall understanding and application of concepts. These prepare students for board examination patterns.
